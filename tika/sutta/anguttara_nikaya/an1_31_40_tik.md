*Pali text — CSCD. Use Simsapa DPD for word lookups (double-click any Pali word).*


### §31-36

> [!abstract]- Atthakathā §31-36
> [[an1_31_40_att#§31|Commentary §31-36]]

(31-36) Catutthassa paṭhame **adanta**nti cittabhāvanāya vinā **na** dantaṃ. Tenāha – **‘‘satisaṃvararahita’’**nti. Catutthe tatiye vuttavipariyāyena attho veditabbo. Pañcamachaṭṭhesu **purimasadisoyevā**ti tatiyacatutthasadiso eva.


### §37-38

> [!abstract]- Atthakathā §37-38
> [[an1_31_40_att#§37-38|Commentary §37-38]]

(37-38) Sattamaṭṭhamesu **upamā panetthā**ti yathā paṭhamādīsu adantahatthiassādayo upamābhāvena gahitā, evamettha satthamaṭṭhamesu **‘‘asaṃvutagharadvārādivasena veditabbā’’**ti vuttaṃ.


### §39-40

> [!abstract]- Atthakathā §39-40
> [[an1_31_40_att#§39-40|Commentary §39-40]]

(39-40) Navamadasamesu **catūhipi padehī**ti adantādīhi catūhi padehi yojetvā navamadasamāni suttāni vuttānīti yojanā.

Adantavaggavaṇṇanā niṭṭhitā.
