### 1. Kimatthiyasuttavaṇṇanā


### §1

(1) Dasakanipātassa paṭhame **avippaṭisāratthānī**ti avippaṭisārappayojanāni. **Avippaṭisārānisaṃsānī**ti avippaṭisārudayāni. Etena avippaṭisāro nāma sīlasssa udayamattaṃ, saṃvaddhitassa rukkhassa chāyāpupphasadisaṃ, añño eva panānena nipphādetabbo samādhiādiguṇoti dasseti. ‘‘Yāva maggāmaggañāṇadassanavisuddhi, tāva taruṇavipassanā’’ti hi vacanato upakkilesavimuttaudayabbayañāṇato paraṃ ayañca vipassanā virajjati yogāvacaro viratto puriso viya bhariyāya saṅkhārato etenāti virāgo.

Kimatthiyasuttavaṇṇanā niṭṭhitā.
