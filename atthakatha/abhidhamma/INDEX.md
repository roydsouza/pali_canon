# Abhidhamma Piṭaka — Atthakathā

**Navigation**: [[INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[atthakatha/INDEX|Atthakathā]]

The primary commentary on the Abhidhamma is the **Atthasālinī** (on the Dhammasaṅgaṇī) by Buddhaghosa, along with the **Sammohavinodanī** (on the Vibhaṅga) and the **Pañcappakaraṇaṭṭhakathā** (on the remaining five books).
