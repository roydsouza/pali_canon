# Therīgāthā-aṭṭhakathā (Paramatthadīpanī) — Commentary

**Navigation**: [[INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[atthakatha/INDEX|Atthakathā]] / [[atthakatha/sutta/INDEX|Sutta]] / [[atthakatha/sutta/khuddaka_nikaya/INDEX|Khuddaka Nikāya]]

The commentary on the Therīgāthā is part of the **Paramatthadīpanī** ("Elucidation of the Ultimate Meaning"), written by Ācariya Dhammapāla (6th century CE). It provides the biographical background and circumstances under which each senior nun (*therī*) uttered their verses.
