## 7. Soceyyasuttavaṇṇanā

*Pali text — CSCD (Chattha Sangayana Tipitaka). Use Simsapa DPD for word lookups (double-click any Pali word).*

### §66

> [!info]- Related Links
> - **Mūla**: [[iti66|ITI66 Mūla]]

Sattame **soceyyānī**ti sucibhāvā. **Kāyasoceyya**nti kāyasucaritaṃ, vacīmanosoceyyānipi vacīmanosucaritāneva. Tathā hi vuttaṃ ‘‘tattha katamaṃ kāyasoceyyaṃ? Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī’’tiādi (a. ni. 3.121-122).

Gāthāyaṃ samucchedavasena pahīnasabbakāyaduccaritattā kāyena sucīti **kāyasuci. Soceyyasampanna**nti paṭippassaddhakilesattā suparisuddhāya soceyyasampattiyā upetaṃ. Sesaṃ vuttanayameva.

Sattamasuttavaṇṇanā niṭṭhitā.
