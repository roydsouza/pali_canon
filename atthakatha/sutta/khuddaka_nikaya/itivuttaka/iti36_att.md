## 9. Dutiyanakuhanasuttavaṇṇanā

*Pali text — CSCD (Chattha Sangayana Tipitaka). Use Simsapa DPD for word lookups (double-click any Pali word).*

### §36

> [!info]- Related Links
> - **Mūla**: [[iti36|ITI36 Mūla]]

Navame **abhiññattha**nti kusalādivibhāgena khandhādivibhāgena ca sabbadhamme abhivisiṭṭhena ñāṇena aviparītato jānanatthaṃ. **Pariññattha**nti tebhūmakadhamme ‘‘idaṃ dukkha’’ntiādinā parijānanatthaṃ samatikkamanatthañca. Tattha abhiññeyyaabhijānanā catusaccavisayā. Pariññeyyaparijānanā pana yadipi dukkhasaccavisayā, pahānasacchikiriyābhāvanābhisamayehi pana vinā na pavattatīti pahānādayopi idha gahitāti veditabbaṃ. Sesaṃ anantarasutte vuttatthameva.

Navamasuttavaṇṇanā niṭṭhitā.
