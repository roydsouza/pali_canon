1. Hiriottappasuttavaṇṇanā


### §65

(65) Sattamassa paṭhame **hatūpaniso**ti hataupaniso chinnapaccayo. **Yathābhūtañāṇadassana**nti taruṇavipassanā. **Nibbidāvirāgo**ti balavavipassanā ceva maggo ca. **Vimuttiñāṇadassana**nti arahattavimutti ca paccavekkhaṇā ca.
