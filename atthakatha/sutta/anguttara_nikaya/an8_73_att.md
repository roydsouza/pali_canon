## 3-9. Maraṇassatisuttadvayādivaṇṇanā

*Pali text — CSCD. Use Simsapa DPD for word lookups (double-click any Pali word).*

### 3-9. Maraṇassatisuttadvayādivaṇṇanā

Tatiye **bhāvetha no**ti bhāvetha nu. **Sāsana**nti anusiṭṭhi. **Āsavānaṃ khayāyā**ti arahattaphalatthāya. Catutthe **patihitāyā**ti paṭipannāya. **So mamassa antarāyo**ti so mama jīvitantarāyopi, puthujjanakālakiriyaṃ karontassa saggantarāyopi maggantarāyopi assa. **Satthakā vā me vātā**ti satthaṃ viya aṅgamaṅgāni kantantīti satthakā. Pañcamādīni vuttanayāneva. Navame **saṃsaggārāmatā**ti pañcavidhe saṃsagge ārāmatā.
