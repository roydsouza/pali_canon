### 5. Anussatiṭṭhānasuttavaṇṇanā


### §25

> [!abstract]- Tīkā §25
> [[an6_25_tik#§25|Sub-commentary §25]]

(25) Pañcame **anussatiṭṭhānānī**ti anussatikāraṇāni. **Itipi so bhagavā**tiādīni visuddhimagge (visuddhi. 1.123 ādayo) vitthāritāneva. **Idampi kho, bhikkhave, ārammaṇaṃ karitvā**ti idaṃ buddhānussatikammaṭṭhānaṃ ārammaṇaṃ karitvā. **Visujjhantī**ti paramavisuddhiṃ nibbānaṃ pāpuṇanti. Sesaṃ sabbattha uttānatthameva. Imasmiṃ pana sutte cha anussatiṭṭhānāni missakāni kathitānīti veditabbāni.
