## 4. Adantavaggavaṇṇanā

*Pali text — CSCD. Use Simsapa DPD for word lookups (double-click any Pali word).*


### §31

> [!abstract]- Tīkā §31
> [[an1_31_40_tik#§31-36|Sub-commentary §31]]

(31) Catutthassa paṭhame **adanta**nti savisevanaṃ adantahatthiassādisadisaṃ. **Citta**nti vaṭṭavasena uppannacittameva.


### §32

> [!abstract]- Tīkā §32
> [[an1_31_40_tik#§31-36|Sub-commentary §32]]

(32) Dutiye **danta**nti nibbisevanaṃ dantahatthiassādisadisaṃ. Imasmimpi suttadvaye vaṭṭavivaṭṭavasena uppannacittameva kathitaṃ. Yathā cettha, evaṃ ito paresupīti.


### §33

> [!abstract]- Tīkā §33
> [[an1_31_40_tik#§31-36|Sub-commentary §33]]

(33) Tatiye **agutta**nti agopitaṃ satisaṃvararahitaṃ aguttahatthiassādisadisaṃ.


### §34

> [!abstract]- Tīkā §34
> [[an1_31_40_tik#§31-36|Sub-commentary §34]]

(34) Catutthe **gutta**nti gopitaṃ avissaṭṭhasatisaṃvaraṃ guttahatthiassādisadisaṃ.


### §35-36

> [!abstract]- Tīkā §35-36
> [[an1_31_40_tik#§31-36|Sub-commentary §35-36]]

(35-36) Pañcamachaṭṭhāni **arakkhitaṃ rakkhita**nti padavasena bujjhanakānaṃ ajjhāsayena vuttāni. Attho panettha purimasadisoyeva.


### §37-38

> [!abstract]- Tīkā §37-38
> [[an1_31_40_tik#§37-38|Sub-commentary §37-38]]

(37-38) Sattamaṭṭhamesupi eseva nayo. Upamā panettha asaṃvutagharadvārādivasena veditabbā.


### §39-40

> [!abstract]- Tīkā §39-40
> [[an1_31_40_tik#§39-40|Sub-commentary §39-40]]

(39-40) Navamadasamāni catūhipi padehi yojetvā vuttāni. Imasmimpi vagge vaṭṭavivaṭṭameva kathitanti.

Adantavaggavaṇṇanā.
