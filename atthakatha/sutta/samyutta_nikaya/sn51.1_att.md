1. Apārasuttavaṇṇanā


### §813

(813) Iddhipādasaṃyuttassa paṭhame chandaṃ nissāya pavatto samādhi **chandasamādhi**. Padhānabhūtā saṅkhārā **padhānasaṅkhārā. Samannāgata**nti tehi dhammehi upetaṃ. Iddhiyā pādaṃ, iddhibhūtaṃ vā pādanti **iddhipādaṃ**. Sesesupi eseva nayo. Ayamettha saṅkhepo, vitthāro pana iddhipādavibhaṅge (vibha. 431 ādayo) āgatova. Visuddhimagge (visuddhi. 2.3.69 ādayo) panassa attho dīpito. Tathā maggabojjhaṅgasatipaṭṭhānasaṃyuttesu ceva idha ca ekaparicchedova.
