# Faith / Conviction (Saddhā)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[five_spiritual_faculties|Five Spiritual Faculties]] / [[five_powers|Five Powers]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Saddhindriyaṃ, saddhābalaṃ: tathāgatassa bodhiṃ saddahati..."
> 
> *“The faculty of faith, the power of faith: one has faith in the awakening of the Tathāgata...”*

---

## Detailed Description

Saddhā is the confidence, trust, or conviction that arises from understanding the Dhamma. It is not blind belief, but the trust in the Buddha's awakening, the efficacy of kamma, and the path of training, which inspires effort.
