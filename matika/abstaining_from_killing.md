# Abstaining from Killing (Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[five_precepts|Five Precepts]] / [[eight_precepts|Eight Precepts]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Pāṇātipātā veramaṇīsikkhāpadaṃ samādiyāmi..."
> 
> *“I undertake the training rule to abstain from taking life...”*

---

## Detailed Description

The first precept of Buddhist ethics. It requires refraining from deliberately destroying, harming, or shortening the life of any living creature. It is motivated by loving-kindness and compassion for all life.
