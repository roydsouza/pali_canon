# Energy / Effort (Viriya)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[seven_awakening_factors|Seven Awakening Factors]] / [[five_spiritual_faculties|Five Spiritual Faculties]] / [[five_powers|Five Powers]] / [[ten_perfections|Ten Perfections]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Viriyasambojjhaṅgo, viriyindriyaṃ, viriyabalaṃ..."
> 
> *“The awakening factor of energy, the faculty of energy, the power of energy...”*

---

## Detailed Description

Viriya is spiritual energy, vigor, and determination. It drives the mind to overcome sloth and dullness, actively strives to cultivate wholesome states, and provides the strength required to sustain meditative practice.
