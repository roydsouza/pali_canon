# The Sangha (Sangha)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[three_refuges|Three Refuges]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Supaṭipanno bhagavato sāvakasaṅgho..."
> 
> *“The community of the Blessed One's disciples has practiced well...”*

---

## Detailed Description

The Sangha is the community of practitioners. Broadly, it refers to the monastic order, and specifically, to the Ariya-Sangha (noble disciples who have attained one of the four stages of stream-entry, once-returning, non-returning, or Arahantship).
