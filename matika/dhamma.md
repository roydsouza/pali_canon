# The Dhamma (Dhamma)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[three_refuges|Three Refuges]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Svākkhāto bhagavatā dhammo sandiṭṭhiko akāliko..."
> 
> *“The Dhamma is well-expounded by the Blessed One, visible here and now, timeless...”*

---

## Detailed Description

The Dhamma is the truth of reality, the natural laws of kamma and liberation, and the teachings expounded by the Buddha. It is described as visible here and now, inviting one to come and see (*ehipassiko*).
