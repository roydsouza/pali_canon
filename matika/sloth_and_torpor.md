# Sloth and Torpor (Thīna-middha)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[five_hindrances|Five Hindrances]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Thīnañca middhañca: thīnaṃ cittass'akalyatā, middhaṃ kāyassa..."
> 
> *“Sloth and torpor: sloth is the sickness of mind, torpor is the sickness of body...”*

---

## Detailed Description

Thīna-middha is the dullness, sluggishness, and sleepiness of mind (sloth) and body (torpor). It manifests as mental fog and lack of energy, and is countered by arousing energy (*viriya*).
