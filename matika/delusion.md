# Delusion / Ignorance (Moha)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[three_unwholesome_roots|Three Unwholesome Roots]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Katamo ca, bhikkhave, moho? Yaṃ aññāṇaṃ..."
> 
> *“And what, bhikkhus, is delusion? The ignorance...”*

---

## Detailed Description

Moha is delusion and ignorance. It is the mental state of confusion and lack of clarity, which misperceives reality and forms the foundation upon which greed and hatred can arise.
