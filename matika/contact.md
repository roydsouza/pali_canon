# Contact / Sensory Impingement (Phassa)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[dependent_origination|Dependent Origination]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Chayime phassakāyā: cakkhusamphasso, sotasamphasso..."
> 
> *“Six classes of contact: eye-contact, ear-contact...”*

---

## Detailed Description

Phassa is the coming together of three things: a sense organ, a sense object, and the corresponding consciousness. Contact is the spark that ignites experience, serving as the necessary condition for feeling (*vedanā*) to arise.
