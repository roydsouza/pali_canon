# Hatred / Anger (Dosa)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[three_unwholesome_roots|Three Unwholesome Roots]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Katamo ca, bhikkhave, doso? Yo cittassa vyāpādo..."
> 
> *“And what, bhikkhus, is hatred? The ill will of the mind...”*

---

## Detailed Description

Dosa is hatred, anger, and aversion. It is the mental state of pushing away, rejecting, and showing hostility toward experiences or people, acting as the root of ill will and conflict.
