# Seven Awakening Factors (Satta Bojjhaṅgā)

**Navigation**: [[INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[matika/INDEX|Mātika]]

## The List

1. **Satisambojjhaṅgo**  
   *Mindfulness*
2. **Dhammavicayasambojjhaṅgo**  
   *Investigation of phenomena*
3. **Viriyasambojjhaṅgo**  
   *Energy*
4. **Pītisambojjhaṅgo**  
   *Rapture*
5. **Passaddhisambojjhaṅgo**  
   *Tranquility*
6. **Samādhisambojjhaṅgo**  
   *Concentration*
7. **Upekkhasambojjhaṅgo**  
   *Equanimity*
