# Purification by Knowledge & Vision of Path (Maggāmaggañāṇadassana-visuddhi)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[seven_purifications|Seven Purifications]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Maggāmaggañāṇadassana... maggāmaggañāṇa..."
> 
> *“Knowledge and vision of what is path and what is not path...”*

---

## Detailed Description

The fifth purification. It is the discernment that distinguishes between the true path of insight and the corruptions of insight (such as attachment to light, joy, or tranquility arising during meditation).
