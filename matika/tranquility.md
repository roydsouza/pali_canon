# Tranquility (Passaddhi)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[seven_awakening_factors|Seven Awakening Factors]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Kāyapassaddhi, cittapassaddhi..."
> 
> *“Tranquility of body, tranquility of mind...”*

---

## Detailed Description

Passaddhi is the physical quietude and mental calm that succeeds joy. It eliminates physical tension (kāya-passaddhi) and mental restlessness (citta-passaddhi), preparing the mind for deep immersion/concentration.
