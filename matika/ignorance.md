# Ignorance (Avijjā)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[dependent_origination|Dependent Origination]] / [[ten_fetters|Ten Fetters]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Dukkhe aññāṇaṃ, samudaye aññāṇaṃ..."
> 
> *“Non-knowledge of suffering, non-knowledge of its origin...”*

---

## Detailed Description

Avijjā is the root cause of saṃsāra and suffering. It is not a mere lack of facts, but the active delusion that misperceives the impermanent as permanent, the unsatisfactory as satisfying, and the non-self as self. It is defined as ignorance of the Four Noble Truths.
