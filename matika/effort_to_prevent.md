# Effort to Prevent (Saṃvara)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[four_right_exertions|Four Right Exertions]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Anuppannānaṃ pāpakānaṃ akusalānaṃ dhammānaṃ anuppādāya..."
> 
> *“To prevent the arising of unarisen evil unwholesome states...”*

---

## Detailed Description

The first right exertion. It involves guarding the sense doors (eyes, ears, etc.) to prevent unwholesome thoughts, desires, and distractions from arising in the mind.
