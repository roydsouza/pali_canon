# Four Sublime States (Cattāro Brahmavihārā)

**Navigation**: [[INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[matika/INDEX|Mātika]]

Also known as the Four Divine Abodes or Immeasurables.

## The List

1. **Mettā**  
   *Loving-kindness (goodwill / benevolence)*
2. **Karuṇā**  
   *Compassion (empathy for suffering)*
3. **Muditā**  
   *Sympathetic joy (appreciation / gladness at others' happiness)*
4. **Upekkhā**  
   *Equanimity (impartiality / peace of mind)*
