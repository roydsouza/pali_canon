# Determination / Resolve (Adhiṭṭhāna)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[ten_perfections|Ten Perfections]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Adhiṭṭhānapāramī: acalādhiṭṭhānaṃ..."
> 
> *“The perfection of determination: immovable resolve...”*

---

## Detailed Description

Adhiṭṭhāna is determination, resolution, and firm purpose. It is the power of resolve that sustains a practitioner through obstacles and long periods of training without wavering or giving up.
