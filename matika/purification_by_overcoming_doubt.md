# Purification by Overcoming Doubt (Kaṅkhāvitaraṇa-visuddhi)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[seven_purifications|Seven Purifications]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Kaṅkhāvitaraṇavisuddhi... nāmarūpānaṃ paccayapariggaha..."
> 
> *“Purification by overcoming doubt... discerning the causes of name and form...”*

---

## Detailed Description

The fourth purification. It is the understanding of the causal relationships of dependent origination, seeing how name-and-form arise due to causes, which resolves all doubt regarding past, present, and future existences.
