# Path to Cessation (Magga)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[four_noble_truths|Four Noble Truths]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Ayameva ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo, seyyathidaṃ—sammādiṭṭhi..."
> 
> *“It is this Noble Eightfold Path, that is: right view...”*

---

## Detailed Description

Magga is the way of practice leading to the cessation of suffering. It is the Noble Eightfold Path (*Ariyo Aṭṭhaṅgiko Maggo*), which is divided into the three trainings: wisdom (*paññā*), ethical conduct (*sīla*), and mental concentration (*samādhi*). It is the middle way between indulgence and self-mortification.
