# Immersion / Concentration (Samādhi)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[seven_awakening_factors|Seven Awakening Factors]] / [[five_spiritual_faculties|Five Spiritual Faculties]] / [[five_powers|Five Powers]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Samādhisambojjhaṅgo, samādhindriyaṃ, samādhibalaṃ..."
> 
> *“The awakening factor of immersion, the faculty of immersion, the power of immersion...”*

---

## Detailed Description

Samādhi is the collectedness, stillness, and one-pointedness of mind. It unifies mental activity, stops wandering thoughts, and leads to the deep meditative absorptions (jhānas), stabilizing the mind for wisdom.
