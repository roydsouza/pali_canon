# Lust for Immaterial Existence (Arūparāga)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[ten_fetters|Ten Fetters]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Arūpabhavesu chandarāgo..."
> 
> *“Lust and desire for immaterial/formless existences...”*

---

## Detailed Description

Arūparāga is the attachment to rebirth in the formless realms, or attachment to the formless meditative attainments (infinite space, infinite consciousness, nothingness, neither-perception-nor-non-perception). It is cut by an Arahant.
