# Truthfulness (Sacca)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[ten_perfections|Ten Perfections]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Saccapāramī: saccavādī, saccasandho..."
> 
> *“The perfection of truthfulness: speaking truth, committing to truth...”*

---

## Detailed Description

Sacca is the perfection of truthfulness, honesty, and alignment with reality. It is the commitment to speak only what is true and beneficial, and to align one's life with the Truth of Dhamma.
