# Abstaining from Sexual Misconduct (Kāmesumicchācārā veramaṇī)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[five_precepts|Five Precepts]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Kāmesumicchācārā veramaṇīsikkhāpadaṃ samādiyāmi..."
> 
> *“I undertake the training rule to abstain from sexual misconduct...”*

---

## Detailed Description

The third precept for lay practitioners. It requires refraining from adultery, exploitation, abuse, or any sexual action that violates trust and commitments. It preserves relational harmony and respect.
