# Compassion (Karuṇā)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[four_sublime_states|Four Sublime States]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Karuṇūpekkhā, karuṇāyanā: paradukkhe sati kampanaṃ..."
> 
> *“Compassion: trembling at the suffering of others...”*

---

## Detailed Description

Karuṇā is compassion. It is the quality of heart that trembles in response to the suffering of others, wishing for them to be free from pain and distress. It counters cruelty and cold indifference.
