# Identity View / Self-view (Sakkāyadiṭṭhi)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[ten_fetters|Ten Fetters]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Idha, bhikkhave, assutavā puthujjanaṃ... rūpaṃ attato samanupassati..."
> 
> *“Here, bhikkhus, an uninstructed worldling... regards form as self...”*

---

## Detailed Description

Sakkāyadiṭṭhi is the fundamental delusion that identifies one's selfhood with one or more of the five aggregates (e.g. regarding form, feeling, perception, choices, or consciousness as 'myself'). It is the first fetter cut at stream-entry.
