# Three Marks of Existence (Tilakkhaṇa)

**Navigation**: [[INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[matika/INDEX|Mātika]]

## The List

1. **Anicca**  
   *Impermanence / Inconstancy*
2. **Dukkha**  
   *Suffering / Unsatisfactoriness*
3. **Anattā**  
   *Non-self / Egolessness*
