# Effort to Abandon (Pahāna)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[four_right_exertions|Four Right Exertions]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Uppannānaṃ pāpakānaṃ akusalānaṃ dhammānaṃ pahānāya..."
> 
> *“To abandon arisen evil unwholesome states...”*

---

## Detailed Description

The second right exertion. It involves actively letting go, dissolving, and banishing unwholesome states (like sensuality, anger, or worry) that have already arisen in the mind.
