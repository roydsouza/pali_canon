# Appreciative / Sympathetic Joy (Muditā)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[four_sublime_states|Four Sublime States]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Modanā, muditā, pramodo: parasampattiyā pamodanaṃ..."
> 
> *“Rejoicing, sympathetic joy: gladness at the success of others...”*

---

## Detailed Description

Muditā is appreciative or sympathetic joy. It is the capacity to rejoice in the happiness, achievements, and success of other living beings, serving as the direct antidote to envy and jealousy.
