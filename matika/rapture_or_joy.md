# Rapture / Joy (Pīti)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[seven_awakening_factors|Seven Awakening Factors]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Pītisambojjhaṅgo: pīti, pāmojjaṃ, hāso..."
> 
> *“The awakening factor of rapture: rapture, gladness, laughter...”*

---

## Detailed Description

Pīti is the physical and mental rapture, joy, or thrill born of meditation. It arises when the mind is secluded from sensory distractions and unwholesome states, providing a pleasant interest that prevents restlessness.
