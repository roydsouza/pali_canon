# Birth (Jāti)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[dependent_origination|Dependent Origination]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Yā tesaṃ tesaṃ sattānaṃ tamhi tamhi sattanikāye jāti sañjāti..."
> 
> *“The birth, conception, production, manifestation of aggregates of various beings...”*

---

## Detailed Description

Jāti is the entry of consciousness into a new physical existence. It is defined as the birth, conception, and manifestation of the aggregates and acquisition of the sense bases in any realm, representing the start of a new cycle of suffering.
