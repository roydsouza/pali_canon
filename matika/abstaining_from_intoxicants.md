# Abstaining from Intoxicants (Surāmerayamajjapamādaṭṭhānā veramaṇī)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[five_precepts|Five Precepts]] / [[eight_precepts|Eight Precepts]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Surāmerayamajjapamādaṭṭhānā veramaṇīsikkhāpadaṃ samādiyāmi..."
> 
> *“I undertake the training rule to abstain from fermented and distilled liquors and intoxicants...”*

---

## Detailed Description

The fifth precept. It requires refraining from alcohol, drugs, or substances that impair mindfulness and cause heedlessness. It safeguards ethical integrity by preserving mental clarity.
