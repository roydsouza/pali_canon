# The Buddha (Buddha)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[three_refuges|Three Refuges]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Itipi so bhagavā arahaṃ sammāsambuddho..."
> 
> *“Thus is the Blessed One: an arahant, a fully awakened one...”*

---

## Detailed Description

The Buddha is the historical teacher, Siddhattha Gotama, who discovered the path to Nibbāna through his own efforts and taught it out of compassion. He represents the teacher and the ultimate potential of human awakening.
