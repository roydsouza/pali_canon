# Generosity (Dāna)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[ten_perfections|Ten Perfections]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Dānapāramī: datvā ca pariccajitvā ca..."
> 
> *“The perfection of generosity: giving and relinquishing...”*

---

## Detailed Description

Dāna is the practice of giving, generosity, and sharing. It counters the root of greed and attachment, cultivates selflessness, and supports the Sangha and those in need. It is the first of the ten perfections.
