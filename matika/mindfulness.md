# Mindfulness (Sati)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[seven_awakening_factors|Seven Awakening Factors]] / [[five_spiritual_faculties|Five Spiritual Faculties]] / [[five_powers|Five Powers]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Satisambojjhaṅgo, satindriyaṃ, satibalaṃ..."
> 
> *“The awakening factor of mindfulness, the faculty of mindfulness, the power of mindfulness...”*

---

## Detailed Description

Sati is the quality of steady, clear, and non-reactive attention. It recollectedly anchors the mind in the present moment, allowing it to monitor experience with clarity, and serves as the foundation for both tranquility and insight.
