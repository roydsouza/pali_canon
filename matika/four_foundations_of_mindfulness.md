# Four Foundations of Mindfulness (Cattāro Satipaṭṭhānā)

**Navigation**: [[INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[matika/INDEX|Mātika]]

## The List

1. **Kāyānupassanā**  
   *Contemplation of the body*
2. **Vedanānupassanā**  
   *Contemplation of feelings / sensations*
3. **Cittānupassanā**  
   *Contemplation of the mind / consciousness*
4. **Dhammānupassanā**  
   *Contemplation of phenomena (dhammas / mind-objects)*
