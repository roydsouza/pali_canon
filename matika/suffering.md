# Suffering / Unsatisfactoriness (Dukkha)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[four_noble_truths|Four Noble Truths]] / [[three_marks|Three Marks]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Cattāri ariyasaccāni: dukkhaṃ ariyasaccaṃ..."
> 
> *“The Four Noble Truths: the noble truth of suffering...”*

---

## Detailed Description

Dukkha is the fundamental unsatisfactoriness of conditioned existence. It encompasses physical pain (dukkha-dukkha), the suffering of change and impermanence (vipariṇāma-dukkha), and the pervasive unsatisfactoriness of conditioned formations (saṅkhāra-dukkha). Understanding dukkha is the first step toward liberation.
