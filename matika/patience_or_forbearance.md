# Patience / Forbearance (Khanti)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[ten_perfections|Ten Perfections]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Khantipāramī: khamano, adhivāsetā..."
> 
> *“The perfection of patience: enduring and forbearing...”*

---

## Detailed Description

Khanti is patience, tolerance, and forbearance. It is the capacity to endure heat, cold, hunger, pain, insults, and harsh words without reacting in anger or losing one's peace of mind.
