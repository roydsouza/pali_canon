# Five Hindrances (Pañca Nīvaraṇāni)

**Navigation**: [[INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[matika/INDEX|Mātika]]

## The List

1. **Kāmacchanda**  
   *Sensual desire*
2. **Byāpāda**  
   *Ill-will*
3. **Thīnamiddha**  
   *Sloth and torpor (lethargy and sluggishness)*
4. **Uddhaccakukkucca**  
   *Restlessness and worry (remorse)*
5. **Vicikicchā**  
   *Skeptical doubt*
