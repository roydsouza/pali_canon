# Ten Perfections (Dasa Pāramī)

**Navigation**: [[INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[matika/INDEX|Mātika]]

## The List

1. **Dāna**  
   *Generosity (giving)*
2. **Sīla**  
   *Virtue (morality / ethical conduct)*
3. **Nekkhamma**  
   *Renunciation (letting go)*
4. **Paññā**  
   *Wisdom (discernment)*
5. **Viriya**  
   *Energy (effort / persistence)*
6. **Khanti**  
   *Patience (forbearance)*
7. **Sacca**  
   *Truthfulness (honesty)*
8. **Adhiṭṭhāna**  
   *Determination (resolution)*
9. **Mettā**  
   *Loving-kindness (goodwill)*
10. **Upekkhā**  
    *Equanimity (impartiality)*
