# Form / Matter (Rūpa)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[five_aggregates|Five Aggregates]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Cattāro ca mahābhūtā, catunnañca mahābhūtānaṃ upādāyarūpaṃ..."
> 
> *“The four great elements and form derived from them...”*

---

## Detailed Description

Rūpa refers to the physical aspect of existence, comprising the four great elements (earth/solidity, water/cohesion, fire/temperature, wind/motion) and physical matter derived from them. It represents the material base of sentient experience.
