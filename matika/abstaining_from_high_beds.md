# Abstaining from High Beds (Uccāsayanamahāsayanā veramaṇī)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[eight_precepts|Eight Precepts]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Uccāsayanamahāsayanā veramaṇīsikkhāpadaṃ samādiyāmi..."
> 
> *“I undertake the training rule to abstain from high and luxurious beds...”*

---

## Detailed Description

The eighth precept. It requires refraining from sleeping on elevated, luxurious, or soft beds that encourage indulgence and sleepiness. It cultivates simplicity, mindfulness, and wakefulness.
