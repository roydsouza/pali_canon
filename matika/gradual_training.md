# Gradual Training (Anupubbasikkhā)

**Navigation**: [[INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[matika/INDEX|Mātika]]

## The Lay Progression (Upāsakānupubba)

1. **Dāna** (Giving / Generosity)  
   *Developing open-handedness, charity, and the joy of sharing as the foundation of merit.*
2. **Sīla** (Ethical Conduct)  
   *Abstaining from harm through the precepts, building self-restraint and freedom from remorse.*
3. **Sagga** (Heavenly Realms)  
   *Reflecting on the peaceful and bright mental states that result from good conduct.*
4. **Kāmānaṃ ādīnava** (Danger of Sensuality)  
   *Recognizing the impermanence, hidden suffering, and instability of pursuing sensual pleasures.*
5. **Nekkhammānisaṃsa** (Benefits of Renunciation)  
   *Understanding the peace, clarity, and mental relief that comes from letting go of attachment.*
6. **Ariyasacca-desanā** (The Four Noble Truths)  
   *Direct insight into suffering, its cause, its ending, and the path, delivered when the mind is ready, receptive, and clear.*

## The Monastic and Deep Practice Progression (Sattha-anupubbasikkhā)

1. **Sīlasampadā** (Accomplishment in Virtue) — Restraint by the training rules.
2. **Indriyasaṃvara** (Restraint of the Senses) — Guarding the sense doors against liking and disliking.
3. **Satisampajañña** (Mindfulness and Situational Awareness) — Awareness in all movements and postures.
4. **Santuṭṭhi** (Contentment) — Satisfaction with simple food, clothing, and shelter.
5. **Nīvaraṇappahāna** (Abandoning the Hindrances) — Sitting in seclusion to clear away the five mental blocks.
6. **Jhānādhigama** (Attainment of Jhāna) — Entering the four states of meditative absorption.
7. **Vipassanā-ñāṇa** (Insight Knowledge) — Seeing the mind-body process as it truly is.
8. **Asavakkhaya** (Ending of the Taints) — Realizing liberation and final freedom.
