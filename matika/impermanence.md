# Impermanence / Inconstancy (Anicca)

**Navigation**: [[../INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[INDEX|Mātika]] / [[three_marks|Three Marks]]

---

## Canonical Definition

> "Yadaniccaṃ taṃ dukkhaṃ..."
> 
> *“What is impermanent is suffering...”*

---

## Detailed Description

Anicca is the mark of impermanence. All conditioned phenomena, physical or mental, are in a state of constant flux, arising and passing away from moment to moment. Clinging to what is impermanent inevitably leads to suffering (*dukkha*).
