# Prosopography — Key Individuals (People)

**Navigation**: [[INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]]

This section profiles the key disciples, contemporaries, and interlocutors of the Buddha who feature prominently in the discourses.

## Key Disciples & Elders (Theras)

*   [[people/sariputta|Sāriputta]] — Foremost in wisdom (*paññā*), the Buddha's chief disciple.
*   [[people/mahamoggallana|Mahāmoggallāna]] — Foremost in psychic power (*iddhi*), the Buddha's second chief disciple.
*   [[people/ananda|Ānanda]] — The Buddha's personal attendant, foremost in hearing (*bahu-suta*) and memory.
*   [[people/mahakassapa|Mahākassapa]] — Foremost in ascetic practices (*dhutaṅga*), president of the First Council.

## Other Characters & Interlocutors

*   [[people/unnabha|Uṇṇābha]] — A brahmin who questioned Ānanda at Kosambī regarding the path and goal.
