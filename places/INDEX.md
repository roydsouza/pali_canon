# Geography — Key Locations (Places)

**Navigation**: [[INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]]

This section explores the primary cities, kingdoms, and monastic sites where the Buddha lived, taught, and established the Sangha.

## The Major Cities of the Middle Country (Majjhimadesa)

*   [[places/savatthi|Sāvatthī]] — Capital of Kosala, where the Buddha spent 25 rains retreats.
*   [[places/rajagaha|Rājagaha]] — Capital of Magadha, surrounded by five hills, site of the First Council.
*   [[places/kosambi|Kosambī]] — Capital of Vatsa (Vaṃsa), situated on the Yamunā river, site of monastic disputes.
*   [[places/vesali|Vesālī]] — Capital of the Licchavis, a republican state, where the Buddha gave his last rains retreat.
