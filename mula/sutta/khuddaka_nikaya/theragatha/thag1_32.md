## THAG1.32: Suppiyatheragāthā — *Suppiya*

### Section 1

**“Ajaraṁ jīramānena,**  
*I’ll swap old age for the unaging,*
**tappamānena nibbutiṁ;**  
*burning for quenching—*
**Nimiyaṁ paramaṁ santiṁ,**  
*the ultimate peace,*
**yogakkhemaṁ anuttaran”ti.**  
*the supreme sanctuary from the yoke.*
*… Suppiyo thero ….*
