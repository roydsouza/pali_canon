## THAG1.27: Lomasakaṅgiyatheragāthā — *Lomasakaṅgiya*

### Section 1

**“Dabbaṁ kusaṁ poṭakilaṁ,**  
*With my chest I’ll thrust aside*
**usīraṁ muñjapabbajaṁ;**  
*the grasses, vines,*
**Urasā panudissāmi,**  
*reeds and creepers,*
**vivekamanubrūhayan”ti.**  
*and foster seclusion.*
*… Lomasakaṅgiyo thero ….*
