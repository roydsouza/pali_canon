## THAG1.49: Rāmaṇeyyakatheragāthā — *Rāmaṇeyyaka*

### Section 1

**“Cihacihābhinadite,**  
*Even with all the sounds,*
**sippikābhirutehi ca;**  
*the chirping and cheeping of the birds,*
**Na me taṁ phandati cittaṁ,**  
*my mind doesn’t waver,*
**ekattanirataṁ hi me”ti.**  
*for I’m devoted to oneness.*
*… Rāmaṇeyyako thero ….*
