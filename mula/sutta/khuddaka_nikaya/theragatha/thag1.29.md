## THAG1.29: Hāritatheragāthā — *Hārita (1st)*

### Section 1

**“Samunnamayamattānaṁ,**  
*Straighten yourself,*
**usukārova tejanaṁ;**  
*like a fletcher straightens an arrow.*
**Cittaṁ ujuṁ karitvāna,**  
*When your mind is upright, Hārita,*
**avijjaṁ bhinda hāritā”ti.**  
*break ignorance to bits!*
*… Hārito thero ….*
