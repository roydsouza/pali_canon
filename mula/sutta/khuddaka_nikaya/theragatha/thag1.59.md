## THAG1.59: Kosalavihāritheragāthā — *Kosalavihārin*

### Section 1

**“Saddhāyāhaṁ pabbajito,**  
*I went forth out of faith*
**araññe me kuṭikā katā;**  
*and built a hut in the wilderness.*
**Appamatto ca ātāpī,**  
*I’m heedful, ardent,*
**sampajāno patissato”ti.**  
*aware, and mindful.*
*… Kosalavihāritthero ….*
