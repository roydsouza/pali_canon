## THAG1.47: Ujjayatheragāthā — *Ujjaya*

### Section 1

**“Namo te buddha vīratthu,**  
*Homage to you, O Buddha, O hero,*
**vippamuttosi sabbadhi;**  
*freed in every way!*
**Tuyhāpadāne viharaṁ,**  
*Meditating in the fruits of your practice,*
**viharāmi anāsavo”ti.**  
*I live without defilements.*
*… Ujjayo thero ….*
