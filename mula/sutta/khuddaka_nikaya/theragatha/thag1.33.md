## THAG1.33: Sopākatheragāthā — *Sopāka (1st)*

### Section 1

**“Yathāpi ekaputtasmiṁ,**  
*Just as a mother would be good*
**piyasmiṁ kusalī siyā;**  
*to her beloved only child;*
**Evaṁ sabbesu pāṇesu,**  
*so, to creatures all and everywhere,*
**sabbattha kusalo siyā”ti.**  
*let one be good.*
*… Sopāko thero ….*
