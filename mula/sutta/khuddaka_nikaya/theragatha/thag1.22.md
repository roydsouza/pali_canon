## THAG1.22: Cittakatheragāthā — *Cittaka*

### Section 1

**“Nīlā sugīvā sikhino,**  
*Crested peacocks with beautiful blue necks*
**Morā kārambhiyaṁ abhinadanti;**  
*cry out in Karaṁvī.*
**Te sītavātakīḷitā,**  
*Stirred by a cool breeze,*
**Suttaṁ jhāyaṁ nibodhentī”ti.**  
*they wake the sleeper to practice absorption.*
*… Cittako thero ….*
