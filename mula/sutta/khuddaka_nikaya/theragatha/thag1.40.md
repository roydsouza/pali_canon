## THAG1.40: Vaḍḍhamānatheragāthā — *Vaḍḍhamāna*

### Section 1

**“Sattiyā viya omaṭṭho,**  
*Like they’re struck by a sword,*
**ḍayhamānova matthake;**  
*like their head was on fire,*
**Bhavarāgappahānāya,**  
*a mendicant should wander mindful,*
**sato bhikkhu paribbaje”ti.**  
*to give up desire for rebirth.*
*… Vaḍḍhamāno thero …*
*vaggo catuttho.*

### Section 2

*Tassuddānaṁ*
*Gahvaratīriyo suppiyo,*
*sopāko ceva posiyo;*
*Sāmaññakāni kumāputto,*
*kumāputtasahāyako;*
*Gavampati tissatthero,*
*vaḍḍhamāno mahāyasoti.*
