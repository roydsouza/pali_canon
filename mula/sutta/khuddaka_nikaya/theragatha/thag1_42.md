## THAG1.42: Khadiravaniyatheragāthā — *Revata of the Acacia Wood*

### Section 1

**“Cāle upacāle sīsūpacāle (…),**  
*Cālā, Upacālā, Sīsupacālā—*
**Patissatā nu kho viharatha;**  
*meditate mindfully!*
**Āgato vo vālaṁ viya vedhī”ti.**  
*I’ve come to you like a hair-splitter.*
*… Khadiravaniyo thero ….*
