## THIG1.6: Dhīrātherīgāthā — *Dhīrā*

### Section 1

**“Dhīre nirodhaṁ phusehi,**  
*Dhīrā, touch cessation,*
**Saññāvūpasamaṁ sukhaṁ;**  
*the blissful settling of perception.*
**Ārādhayāhi nibbānaṁ,**  
*Win extinguishment,*
**Yogakkhemamanuttaran”ti.**  
*the supreme sanctuary from the yoke.*
