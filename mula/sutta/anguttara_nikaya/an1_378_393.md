## 28. Pasādakaradhammavagga
*The Chapter on Inspiring Qualities*

> [!info]- Related Commentary & Sub-commentary
> - **Commentary (Atthakathā)**: [[an1_378_393_att#28. Pasādakaradhammavaggavaṇṇanā|28. Pasādakaradhammavagga (Commentary)]]
> - **Sub-commentary (Ṭīkā)**: [[an1_378_393_tik#28. Pasādakaradhammavaggavaṇṇanāṭīkā|28. Pasādakaradhammavagga (Sub-commentary)]]


**“Addhamidaṁ, bhikkhave, lābhānaṁ yadidaṁ āraññikattaṁ …pe…**  
*“Mendicants, this is definitely something worth having, that is, living in the wilderness …*

**piṇḍapātikattaṁ …**  
*eating only almsfood …*

**paṁsukūlikattaṁ …**  
*wearing rag robes …*

**tecīvarikattaṁ …**  
*having just three robes …*

**dhammakathikattaṁ …**  
*teaching Dhamma …*

**vinayadharattaṁ …**  
*memorizing the monastic law …*

**bāhusaccaṁ …**  
*being very learned …*

**thāvareyyaṁ …**  
*being respected …*

**ākappasampadā …**  
*being well-presented …*

**parivārasampadā …**  
*having a following …*

**mahāparivāratā …**  
*having a large following …*

**kolaputti …**  
*coming from a good family …*

**vaṇṇapokkharatā …**  
*being handsome …*

**kalyāṇavākkaraṇatā …**  
*being one who enunciates well …*

**appicchatā …**  
*having few wishes …*

**appābādhatā”ti.**  
*being healthy.”*

*(Soḷasa pasādakaradhammā niṭṭhitā.)*
