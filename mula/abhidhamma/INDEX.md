# Abhidhamma Piṭaka — Mūla

**Navigation**: [[INDEX|Pali Canon Vault]] / [[mula/INDEX|Mūla]]

The Abhidhamma Piṭaka ("Higher Teaching Basket") is the third piṭaka of the Tipiṭaka, comprising seven books of systematic philosophical analysis.

## The Seven Books (Satta Pakaraṇāni)

| Abbrev | Title | Subject |
|---|---|---|
| Dhs | Dhammasaṅgaṇī | Enumeration of phenomena |
| Vbh | Vibhaṅga | Analysis of phenomena |
| Dhk | Dhātukathā | Discussion of elements |
| Pp | Puggalapaññatti | Description of persons |
| Kv | Kathāvatthu | Points of controversy |
| Yam | Yamaka | Book of pairs |
| Paṭṭh | Paṭṭhāna | Conditional relations |
