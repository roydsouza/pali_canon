## Memorization Strategy

1. **Aural Familiarity**: Listen to high-quality recordings of the chants (e.g., from Amaravati or Bhante Sujato) to internalize the correct Pali cadence and pronunciation.
2. **Interleaved Recall**: Do not repeat one verse 50 times sequentially. Instead, read it once, close your eyes and recall it, then move to another verse, and come back.
3. **Daily Recitation**: Recite the *Tisarana* and your active verses at the start of every meditation session to compose the mind.
